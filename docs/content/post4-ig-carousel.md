# POST 4 — Instagram Carousel: "Progress Report"

**Platform:** Instagram  
**Post date:** Wednesday Mar 12, 6:00 PM EST  
**Type:** Carousel (5 slides)  
**Tool:** Canva (free tier works fine)

---

## Canva Setup

1. Go to canva.com → Create a design → "Instagram Post" (1080x1080)
2. Pick a consistent template style:
   - **Background:** Warm cream/beige (#FFF8F0) or soft warm white
   - **Font:** One clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or Poppins)
   - **Accent color:** Warm gold (#D4A853) or soft orange (#E8915A)
   - **Corner detail:** Small "hangintherepunch.com" in bottom-right of EVERY slide (small, ~10pt, gray text)
3. Design all 5 slides in one multi-page Canva project
4. Export as PNG (all pages)

## Pro tips:
- The "Save this" CTA in the caption is important — saves are the #1 signal Instagram's algorithm uses to boost carousel posts
- Carousels get shown to non-followers more than single images
- Keep text large enough to read on mobile without zooming
