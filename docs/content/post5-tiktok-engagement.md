## Engagement bait (the real strategy):

This post is designed to generate **comments**. The "name a character with a better redemption arc" format is an invitation to reply. Expect:
- "no because this is actually so real"
- "the plushie 😭"
- "I literally cannot name one"
- People tagging friends

**When comments come in, REPLY to the top ones:**
- Heart the first 20-30 comments within the first hour
- Reply to 3-5 with something like "right?? the plushie era was everything" or "he's literally unstoppable now"
- Pin the best comment
